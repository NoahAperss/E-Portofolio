Stage Elmos

In the past three months, I have been working as an intern at Elmos NV, a well-established and reputable company in the field of IT consultancy. My primary responsibility during this internship has been to contribute to the development of a cutting-edge application that collects and consolidates social media data from various sources, ultimately presenting it in the form of accessible, user-friendly dashboards. While there are already existing applications that serve a similar purpose, our aim is to offer a more cost-effective solution, ensuring that our clients are not burdened with exorbitant monthly fees, such as the $1,000 that other services may charge.

Technologies and My Role

In order to facilitate this process, we have chosen to store the gathered social media data in a BigQuery database. This approach allows for seamless integration with Power BI, empowering our clients to create their own customized dashboards, which can offer even more detailed and specific insights. To accomplish our objectives, we have divided the project into four distinct components: one frontend and three interconnected backends, each responsible for a different aspect of the process. During my time at Elmos NV, I have been primarily involved in the development of the authentication backend.

Learning and Contributions

This has allowed me to learn numerous new concepts and skills, such as devising a workflow for connecting our application's users to their LinkedIn accounts through OAuth. Additionally, I have been responsible for creating a secure route that only our other backends can access, incorporating a signature into the route and ensuring that it is only accessible with the correct key and header. The authentication backend also required me to develop a comprehensive user/organization and JWT system, including the implementation of roles, statuses, and other related features. Aside from my work on the backend, I have also contributed to the creation of data models by participating in brainstorming sessions and group meetings.

Agile Methodology and Communication

Throughout the duration of this project, our team has adopted an agile methodology, working within the framework of sprints and completing assigned tasks within specified timeframes. We have maintained regular communication through daily meetings at 10 AM via Microsoft Teams, ensuring that we stay aligned and focused on our objectives.
